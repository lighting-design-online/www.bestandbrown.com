Luxury Lighting by Hinkley Lighting

Now in their fourth generation of family leadership, this innovative and chic lighting company has come a long way since making small outdoor lanterns in 1922. Today, there is no doubt that Hinkley products follow distinctive trends with the ambition for their products to make a clear decorative statement with the highest of quality and durability. 

Hinkley continue to produce an extensive range of outdoor lighting solutions, paying homage to where the company started. With a huge array of finishes to add to their high quality products and online interactive support, Hinkley pride themselves on putting their customers first, no matter the size of the project or where in the world the customer may be.
