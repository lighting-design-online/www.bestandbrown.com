Brands and manufacturers

Luxury lighting brands worth exploring

The brands page is the right place to compare the makers behind the fittings. It helps you build a shortlist around a finish language, a family of shapes or a consistent approach to quality.

Use it to move from luxury lighting inspiration into a more deliberate designer lighting specification.

How to compare lighting brands

Compare the family

Strong brands usually repeat a visual language across ceiling, wall and portable fittings.

  • Look for shared proportions.
  • Check how the finish translates between rooms.
  • Use one maker to keep the scheme coherent.

Check the details

Quality lighting is usually visible in the edge detail, material choice and the way the fitting sits in the room.

  • Review the silhouette in daylight.
  • Compare metal, glass and shade combinations.
  • Keep the fit with the architecture in mind.

Use brand pages well

Move from the brand hub into category pages to refine the shortlist for the actual room.

  • Start with the maker.
  • Move into the room category.
  • Finish with the most beautiful lighting option.
